In participating in the Hotels.ng, (HNG) Internship, one is required to join the learning-team of the organisation where lessons are undertaken on javascript, cascade stylesheet, hyper text markup language and git using the codecademy's syllabus.

Upon completion of the lessons, one is required to undertake three (3) projects out of four available projects like; building of a digital clock, calculator, word counter and random character generator.

In order for you to view my projects, you will kindly need to enable your javascript if disabled or switch over to a javascript-enabled browser.

To know if your browser supports javascript and it is enabled, visit WhatIsMyBrowser.com.
